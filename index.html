Features

A Pumped Breast Milk Management system for Neonatal ICUs to improve nurse productivity
and increase expression of breast milk in lactating mothers

Self-measurement Magic

A fully-automated system to track milk-bottle inventory, recipes, feeding-order, etc. right from your mobile-device.

Workflow Notifications & Alerts

Let the system handle first-in-first-out (FIFO) bottle flow, expiry dates notifications, misfeed alerts, and more...

Rich media-sharing platform

Rich media-sharing platform for nurses to share photos and videos of NICU babies with their mothers.

Production Analytics for Lactation Experts

Real time insights into milk-production patterns of lactating mothers.

Benefits

Better Critical Care, Healthier Babies, Happier Moms

Improve Nurse Productivity

Lact-o-Log can save upto 6.5 full-time nurse hours (13,520 hrs/yr) at an average sized NICU.

Shorten length-of-stay (LOS) for NICU babies

The media sharing platform emulates several natural stimuli for a lactating mom, resulting in higher breast milk production and supply at the NICU.

Move the needle on your HCAHPS score

Create better patient experiences - quickly and effectively - without large capital investments.
